# Underwriter Guide — Reviewing Referred Quotes

## What is the review queue?
When a quote is **referred**, it lands in the underwriter queue for a human decision. Underwriters see referred quotes from **all** brokers.

## What's on the underwriter Overview?
The Review Queue overview shows:
- **KPI cards** — pending review count, premium at risk (the total annual premium of referred quotes), approval rate, and total reviewed.
- **Analytics** — decisions over the last 6 months (approved vs declined), approval rate with a split bar, top referral reasons across the pending queue, and pending exposure.
- **Pending Review** — the oldest 5 referrals first (most urgent), each with an aging badge.
- **Recently Reviewed** — your latest 5 decisions.

## Where do I see all pending or all reviewed?
- **Pending** — the full queue of referrals awaiting a decision, oldest first, with aging badges and a search box (by applicant name or policy).
- **All Reviews** — every decision made, most recent first, searchable by applicant name or policy.

## Why is the queue sorted oldest-first?
So the longest-waiting referrals stay at the top and don't breach service expectations. Aging badges show how long each has waited (e.g. "Today", "2d", or red for 5+ days).

## How do I review a referral?
Open the referred quote. You'll see the full application, the premium, and the specific reasons it was referred. Add an optional review note (shared with the broker on approval) and choose **Approve** or **Decline**.

## What is the AI recommendation?
On a referred quote you can click **Get AI Recommendation**. An AI underwriter reads the full application and the referral reasons and returns an advisory verdict — approve or decline, with a confidence level and specific reasons — and pre-fills your review note. It's **advisory only**: you make the final decision. See "AI Underwriter" for details.

## What happens after I approve or decline?
- **Approve** — the quote becomes available for the broker to bind, the broker is emailed, and it appears in their Action Required. The decision, reviewer, time, and note are recorded.
- **Decline** — the quote is marked declined with your note.

## Can underwriters see quotes that weren't referred?
Underwriters focus on referred quotes. The All Policies search for underwriters is scoped to bound policies only.
