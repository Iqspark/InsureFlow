# Customer Self-Service Portal

This is the **customer** portal — a no-login page for the insured person to view their own policy. It is separate from the **broker portal** (the signed-in dashboard brokers, underwriters, and admins use). For broker-portal questions, see "Portal FAQ" and the "Broker Guide."

## What is the customer portal?
When a policy is bound, the customer is emailed a secure link to their own portal page (`/portal/<token>`). They can open it without logging in to view and manage their policy at a glance.

## Do I need a login or account?
No. The portal is public and tied to your specific policy by a secure token in the link. Don't share the link — anyone with it can view your policy.

## What can I do in the customer portal?
- **See your policy status** — Active, Awaiting payment, or Cancelled.
- **Review the details** — premium, coverage amount, policy term (effective and expiry dates), and the risk/property details, including a map for property policies.
- **Download your policy document** — a branded PDF of your policy.
- **Pay** — if the policy isn't paid yet, a payment button takes you to the secure checkout.
- **Request a change** — submit a "Request a Change" message that goes straight to your broker (not available once a policy is cancelled).

## How do I download my policy document?
Open your portal link and use the **Download** option to get your policy PDF. The same PDF is also attached to your payment receipt email.

## How does "Request a Change" work?
Type what you'd like to change (for example a coverage amount or a contact detail) and submit it. Your broker receives the request by email and follows up. The portal records that the request was made; the broker makes any actual policy change.

## My portal link doesn't work or says "This link has expired."
Portal links **expire 30 days after they're issued**. If your link has expired or won't open, ask your broker to **resend** it — they issue a fresh link with a new 30-day window.

## Is the customer portal the same as where my broker logs in?
No. Your broker, underwriters, and admins use a separate, password-protected portal to manage many policies. Your customer portal link shows only your own policy and needs no login.

## Who do I contact with questions?
Your assigned broker is your point of contact for anything about your quote, policy, payment, or a change request.
