# Getting Started with the InsureFlow Portal

## What is InsureFlow?
InsureFlow is a multi-product insurance broker portal. Brokers walk an applicant through a chat-style questionnaire with a virtual broker ("Alex"), get an instant Accept / Decline / Refer decision with a premium breakdown, save the quote, bind it as a policy, and email the customer a secure payment link. Underwriters review referred quotes, and admins oversee the whole book and manage users.

## What products can I quote?
Ten products today: Vacant Home, Rental Home, Farm, Jeweller's Block, Cyber Liability, Contractor, Architects & Engineers, Retailers, Personal Items, and Lithium Battery Product Liability. Each has its own application questions, pricing, and underwriting rules, but the chat experience, saving, PDF, and result screens are shared.

## What are the roles?
- **Broker** — quotes, binds, and manages their own clients and policies.
- **Underwriter** — reviews referred quotes from all brokers and approves or declines them.
- **Admin** — full access plus portfolio analytics and user management.

## How do I sign in?
Go to the login page and enter your email and password. The demo accounts (one tap each on the login screen) are Broker, Underwriter, and Admin — all with the password `Demo1234!`.

## How do I navigate?
The top navigation changes by role:
- **Broker:** Dashboard · New Quote · Policies · Customers · Search
- **Underwriter:** Review Queue · Pending · All Reviews · All Policies
- **Admin:** Admin · Pending · All Reviews · Customers · All Policies · Users

## What is the quote lifecycle?
1. **Quote** — answer the questions and get an instant decision.
2. **Refer** (if needed) — borderline risks route to an underwriter.
3. **Policy** — pressing *Buy This Policy* binds the quote and emails the applicant a payment link.
4. **Paid** — the customer pays on a secure page and the policy activates.
5. **Renewal** — each policy carries a 12-month term and surfaces in Upcoming Renewals.

## Where do I get help?
Use the Help Navigator chat widget in the bottom corner of any page — it answers questions about products, coverage, pricing, underwriting, and how to use the portal.
