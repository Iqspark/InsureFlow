# Admin Guide — Portfolio Oversight & User Management

## What can an admin do?
An admin has full visibility across all brokers plus user management. Admins can also review referred quotes (Pending and All Reviews) like an underwriter.

## What's on the admin dashboard?
A portfolio view across the whole book:
- **KPI cards** — total quotes, premium bound, paid policies, pending referrals, and total users.
- **Analytics** — premium volume over 6 months, decisions across all brokers (acceptance rate + split), product mix, and **top brokers by premium**.
- **Export CSV** — download the entire portfolio.
- **Recent Activity** — the latest submissions across all brokers, with a link to view all.

## How do I manage users?
Go to **Users**. You can:
- **Add a user** — name, email, temporary password, and role (Admin, Broker, or Underwriter).
- **Change a role** — pick a different role from the dropdown.
- **Activate / deactivate** — toggle a user's access.
- **Reset password** — set a new temporary password (or generate one) inline; share it with the user, who can change it after signing in.
- **Search** — filter users by name, email, or role.

## Are there safeguards on user management?
Yes — you cannot remove the last active admin, and you cannot deactivate your own account.

## What are the roles and what does each see?
- **Broker** — own quotes, policies, and customers.
- **Underwriter** — referred quotes from all brokers (review queue, pending, all reviews) and bound policies.
- **Admin** — everything above, plus portfolio analytics and user management.

## Where does the analytics data come from?
All figures are computed live from the submissions in the system — decisions, premiums, products, brokers, and payment status — so the dashboard always reflects the current book.
